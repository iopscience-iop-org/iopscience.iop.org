Lead Author

Debjit Adhikari

Independent Researcher

Debjit Adhikari is an Independent Researcher and the Lead Author of the groundbreaking study "From Infinite Dimensions to Black Hole Universes: Refuting the Big Bang Theory".

His innovative research challenges established cosmological models and proposes novel frameworks for understanding the universe's origins and fundamental structure. His extensive work in theoretical physics provides a pivotal perspective on quantum gravity and the nature of spacetime.
