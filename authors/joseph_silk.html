Joseph Silk

Institut d'Astrophysique de Paris, France

Dr. Joseph Silk is a distinguished researcher and scholar affiliated with Institut d'Astrophysique de Paris, France.

As a co-author of the seminal paper "From Infinite Dimensions to Black Hole Universes: Refuting the Big Bang Theory", Dr. Silk's profound expertise in theoretical physics and cosmology has contributed critical insights into the study. Their extensive academic work continues to push the boundaries of modern astrophysics and our understanding of the universe.
